Steps
=====
1. For each of the following files, append the prefix "tFTR"

	Parser.vb		becomes tFTRParser.vb
	ParseTree.vb		becomes tFTRParseTree.vb
	Scanner.vb		becomes tFTRScanner.vb
	TextHighlighter.vb	becomes tFTRTextHighlighter.vb

2. Move the three files (above) to \Boston\Versions\SVN\Boston\ClassesParserFTR\TinyPG
	(write over existing files)

3. If Boston is open in Visual Studio do the following:

	Click [Yes to All] to the message box reading: "This file has been modified outside the source editor. Do you want to reload it?"

4. Remove the unused variables that appear in the ErrorList of the Project (for completeness)
