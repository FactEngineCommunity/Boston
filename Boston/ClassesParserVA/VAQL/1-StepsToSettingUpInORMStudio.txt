Steps
=====
1. For each of the following files, append the prefix "tVAQL"

	Parser.vb		becomes tORMQLParser.vb
	ParseTree.vb		becomes tORMQLParseTree.vb
	Scanner.vb		becomes tORMQLScanner.vb
	TextHighlighter.vb	becomes tORMQLTextHighlighter.vb

2. Move the three files (above) to \Bradford\Versions\SVN\Bradford\Classes\Database\ORMQLParser\TinyPG
	(write over existing files)

3. If Bradford is open in Visual Studio do the following:

	Click [Yes to All] to the message box reading: "This file has been modified outside the source editor. Do you want to reload it?"

4. Remove the unused variables that appear in the ErrorList of the Project (for completeness)
