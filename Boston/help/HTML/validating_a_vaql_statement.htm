Validating a VAQL Statement

 Top  Previous  Next

The Virtual Analyst is designed to make the process of entering the details of a model into Boston as easy as possible. The process of validating a VAQL statement is automated by colour-coding the VAQL statement that you have typed into the Virtual Analyst Toolbox once the VAQL statement is complete and has automatically been validated by the Virtual Analyst.

 

The following step/s outline how to initiate the automatic validation of a VAQL statement:

 

1.Press the [Space] bar on your keyboard once you have completed typing in a VAQL statement.
The Virtual Analyst will automatically validate the VAQL statement and colour-code the VAQL Statement to signify that the statement is valid.
