Role Constraints

 

Role Constraints constrain the values that may appear within the object holes of predicates, or Fact Types.

 

For instance, the following ORM Diagram has two Fact Types, 'PersonHasFisrtName' and 'PersonHasLastName'. The External Uniqueness constraint which links to Roles of those Fact Types constrains the values of FistName and LastName of a Person entity to being unique amongst all entities within the Universe of Discourse (UoD).
