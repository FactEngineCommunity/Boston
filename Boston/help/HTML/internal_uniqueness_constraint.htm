Internal Uniqueness Constraints spanning multiple Roles

 Top  Previous  Next

The following steps outline how to add an Internal Uniqueness Constraint to a Fact Type.

 

1.Select the Roles that the Internal Uniqueness Constraint will span;
2.Right-click on a selected Role.
You will be presented with the Role context menu.
 

 
3.Select the [Add Uniqueness Constraint] menu option.
The Internal Uniqueness Constraint will be added to the Fact Type.
