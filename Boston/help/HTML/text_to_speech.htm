There are multiple ways to turn Text-to-Speech on and off for the Virtual Analyst.

 

Turning Text-to-Speech On

 

The easiest way to turn on Text-to-Speech for the Virtual Analyst is to type any of the following commands into the Virtual Analyst Toolbox:

 

       "quietmodeoff"

       "speak to me"

 

Turning Text-to-Speech Off

 

The easiest way to turn off Text-to-Speech for the Virtual Analyst is to type any of the following commands into the Virtual Analyst Toolbox:

 

       "quietmodeon"

       "shoosh"

       "be quiet"
