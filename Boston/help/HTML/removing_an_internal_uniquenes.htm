Removing an Internal Uniqueness Constraint

 Top  Previous  Next

The steps below outline how to remove an Internal Uniqueness Constraint from a Page and Model.

 

NB You cannot remove an Internal Uniqueness Constraint from a Page without removing the Internal Uniqueness Constraint from the underlying Model.

 

1.Select the Internal Uniqueness Constraint by left-clicking on the constraint;
2.Right-click on the Internal Uniqueness Constraint once it has been selected;
You will be presented with the Internal Uniqueness Constraint context menu.
3.Select the [Remove from Page & Model] menu option from the Internal Uniqueness Constraint context menu.
The Internal Uniqueness Constraint will be removed from the Page and Model.
